How to Play

  • Build: Click a building from the menu and place it on the map
  • Resources: Gold mines and elixir collectors generate resources over time
  • Defense: Build cannons and archer towers to defend your base
  • Train: Use elixir to train barbarians and archers
  • Attack: Click Attack Mode to deploy troops and attack buildings
  • Objective: Build a strong base and train an army!
